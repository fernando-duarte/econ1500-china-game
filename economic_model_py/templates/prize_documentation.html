China Growth Game: Prize System

The prize system rewards teams for achieving specific economic milestones and implementing successful policies.

Prizes provide both immediate benefits and long-term advantages. Some prizes make your economy more resilient to negative events, while others boost your growth potential.

GDP Growth Achievement

Awarded to teams that achieve exceptional GDP growth over a sustained period.

Criteria: Maintain GDP growth rate above 8% for at least 3 consecutive rounds.
Effects:
  • TFP increase: +5%
  • Exports multiplier: 1.1x
Historical Context: China maintained double-digit GDP growth rates through much of the 1990s and 2000s.

Technology Leadership

Awarded to teams that achieve technological breakthroughs through R&D investment.

Criteria: Invest at least 2.5% of GDP in R&D for 3 consecutive rounds.
Effects:
  • TFP increase: +8%
  • Tech breakthrough probability: +20%
Historical Context: China's transition from 'Made in China' to 'Created in China' has been supported by massive increases in R&D spending.

Sustainable Growth

Awarded to teams that maintain balanced and sustainable economic growth.

Criteria: Maintain positive GDP growth for 5 consecutive rounds with low volatility (standard deviation < 2%).
Effects:
  • GDP growth stability: +30%
  • Foreign investment multiplier: 1.15x
Historical Context: China's economic planners have emphasized stable growth ('稳定增长') as a key policy objective.

Crisis Management

Awarded to teams that effectively navigate economic crises.

Criteria: Recover to pre-crisis GDP levels within 2 rounds after a major economic shock.
Effects:
  • Recession recovery boost: +25%
  • Capital protection: +20%
Historical Context: China's response to the 2008 global financial crisis included a massive stimulus package.

Export Champion

Awarded to teams that achieve exceptional export growth.

Criteria: Maintain export growth above 10% for 3 consecutive rounds and achieve exports > 30% of GDP.
Effects:
  • Foreign income multiplier: 1.2x
  • Exchange rate advantage: +15%
Historical Context: China's export-led growth strategy transformed it into the world's largest exporter.

General Prize Information

  • Prizes are evaluated at the end of each round.
  • Multiple prizes can be awarded to the same team.
  • Prize effects are permanent and cumulative.
  • Prizes provide both immediate benefits and long-term advantages.
  • Some prizes make your economy more resilient to negative events.

Strategy Tips

Different prizes require different strategies. Here are some general tips:

  • Diversify your approach: Don't focus exclusively on one prize. A balanced strategy can help you achieve multiple prizes.
  • Plan ahead: Some prizes require consistent performance over multiple rounds. Plan your policies accordingly.
  • Adapt to events: Major events like the WTO accession or financial crisis can provide opportunities to demonstrate crisis management skills.
  • Monitor your progress: Keep track of your performance relative to prize criteria to know how close you are to earning each prize.
